Dubai

Dubai, the second-largest emirate in the UAE, is a global metropolis known for its iconic skyline, led by the Burj Khalifa. Under the leadership of Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, the city has diversified its economy beyond oil, becoming a major trade, finance, and tourism hub.

Balancing modernity with tradition, Dubai features cultural landmarks like the Dubai Opera House and the Dubai Museum. It's a melting pot of diverse cultures, creating a vibrant and cosmopolitan atmosphere.

Sustainability is a key focus for the city, evident in initiatives such as the Dubai Clean Energy Strategy 2050 and the Dubai Expo 2020, showcasing Dubai's commitment to a sustainable and forward-looking future.
